Smart Draft Advisor

Real-time pick recommendations based on roster needs, positional scarcity, ADP value, and last year's stats. Runs entirely on your device — no internet required.

📊

Pick Value Grading

Every pick is graded instantly. Color-coded indicators make it easy to spot steals and reaches at a glance.

🏆

Elite Player Tracking

Top 5 players at each position are tagged as Elite with a purple trophy badge. Know when game-changing talent is on the board.

📋

Player Detail Cards

Tap any player for their full card: stats with TD totals, injury status, ADP, ESPN profile, team depth chart, and advisor score.

🎯

Position Requirements

Set min/max roster limits per position. The app tracks your roster and warns you when positions need attention.

📈

League-Wide Analytics

Curve-based grades (A+ to F) for every team after the draft. Compare value picks, reaches, and roster balance.

⏱️

Draft Clock

Built-in stopwatch tracks time per pick. Keep your draft moving with a visible timer and haptic feedback.

⭐

Favorites & Filters

Mark players as favorites. Filter by position, team, or favorites. Sort by overall rank or ADP.

🔄

Keeper League Support

Configure 1-10 forced linear rounds for keeper leagues. Serpentine kicks in after your keeper rounds.
