# User Guide: New Features in Version 3.3

## What's New? 🎉

Version 3.3 brings exciting new features to enhance your draft experience!

## 1. Draft Board Visualization

### What is it?
A visual representation of your draft progress showing all picks in an easy-to-read format.

### Where to find it
Look for the "Draft Board" card on your main draft screen, right below the league information.

### How to use it
- **Colored circles** show pick status:
  - 🔵 Blue = Already picked
  - 🟢 Green = Current pick (that's you!)
  - ⚪ Gray = Future picks
- **Scroll left/right** to see more picks
- **Tap "View Full"** to see complete draft history

### Why it's useful
- See at a glance where you are in the draft
- Understand the draft flow
- Track progress through rounds

## 2. Upcoming Picks

### What is it?
Shows your next 3 picks so you can plan ahead.

### Where to find it
In the Draft Board card, look for "Your Upcoming Picks"

### Example
```
Your Upcoming Picks
Picks: 12, 25, 38
```

### Why it's useful
- Plan your strategy in advance
- Know when you're on the clock next
- Prepare your target players

## 3. Position Needs

### What is it?
Smart recommendations showing which positions you still need to draft.

### Where to find it
In the Draft Board card, look for "Position Needs" with colored chips

### How it works
The app tracks your roster and reminds you when you need:
- At least 1 QB
- At least 2 RBs
- At least 2 WRs
- At least 1 TE
- At least 1 K
- At least 1 DST

### Why it's useful
- Never forget essential positions
- Maintain a balanced roster
- Avoid last-minute panic picks

## 4. Draft Grade & Analytics

### What is it?
A comprehensive report card for your draft performance, shown automatically when the draft completes.

### What you'll see

#### Your Grade
- Letter grade (A+ to F)
- Numerical score (0-100)
- Color-coded: Green = Great, Blue = Good, Orange = OK, Red = Needs Work

#### Draft Strategy
Identifies your approach:
- **RB Heavy**: You loaded up on running backs
- **WR Heavy**: You prioritized wide receivers
- **Zero RB**: You waited on running backs
- **Balanced**: Even distribution across positions
- **QB Stacking**: Multiple quarterbacks
- **Flexible**: Varied approach

#### Key Stats
- **Value Picks**: Players you got below their average draft position (good!)
- **Reach Picks**: Players you drafted above their average position (risky!)
- **Average ADP Difference**: Overall draft value

#### Notable Picks
- 🏆 **Best Value**: Your steal of the draft
- ⚠️ **Biggest Reach**: Your riskiest pick (if any)

#### Position Breakdown
Visual chart showing how many players you drafted at each position

### How to use it
1. Review your grade and strategy
2. Learn from value picks and reaches
3. Share your results with friends
4. View full draft history for details

### Sharing Your Results
Tap "Share Results" to post your draft grade to:
- Twitter
- Facebook
- Text message
- Any app that accepts text

## 5. Improved Accessibility

### What changed?
- **Bigger buttons**: Easier to tap, especially on small screens
- **Screen reader support**: Full compatibility with TalkBack
- **Better feedback**: Clear messages when things are loading

### Who benefits?
- Everyone! Bigger buttons are easier for all users
- Users with visual impairments
- Users with motor difficulties
- Anyone using a screen reader

### How to use screen reader
1. Enable TalkBack in Settings > Accessibility
2. Navigate the app with swipe gestures
3. All buttons and information will be read aloud

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I see other teams' upcoming picks?
A: Currently, the app shows only your upcoming picks. Full draft board shows all picks.

### Q: How is the draft grade calculated?
A: Based on multiple factors:
- Value picks (drafted below ADP)
- Reach picks (drafted above ADP)
- Roster balance
- Average draft position difference

### Q: Can I customize position needs?
A: Currently uses standard roster requirements. Custom leagues coming in future updates!

### Q: What if I disagree with my grade?
A: Grades are based on average draft position data. Your league scoring and strategy may differ!

### Q: Can I view analytics for other teams?
A: Currently shows only your team. League-wide analytics coming soon!

### Q: Does the draft board work offline?
A: Yes! All features work offline once player data is loaded.

## Troubleshooting

### Draft board not showing
- Make sure you've started a draft
- Check that you have teams configured
- Try scrolling down on the draft screen

### Position needs not updating
- Ensure picks are being recorded
- Check that player positions are correct
- Try refreshing the draft screen

### Analytics not appearing
- Only shows when draft is complete
- Make sure all rounds are finished
- Check that picks were recorded

### Share not working
- Ensure you have sharing apps installed
- Check app permissions
- Try a different sharing method
